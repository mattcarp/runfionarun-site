Features
Everything you need on race day
And the weeks of training before it. Built for Gozo's terrain, tested on the actual course.
📍

Live GPS Tracking

Your real-time position on the official 21.1km race route. Satellite map with your exact location through every village and coastal stretch.

🗣️

Voice KM Alerts

Spoken updates at every kilometre — pace, distance, elevation gained. Keep your phone in your pocket. We'll tell you everything you need to hear.

👀

Spectator Mode

Friends and family track you live. They see your dot on the map, your pace, your progress ring — and they can send you a cheer when the hills hit.

📊

Elevation Profile

Interactive chart showing every climb from sea level to 144m. Know what's ahead. The hills of Gozo don't surprise you twice.

🎨

4 Beautiful Themes

Dark Cyan for dawn, Light Coral for golden hour, Spectator Dark for the crowd. Switch live — no restart, instant vibes.

🗺️

Full POI Layer

Water stations, first aid, marshals, parking, music stages, toilets, and every turn direction — all on the map before you lace up.

Made with love in Gozo

This app was born in Xagħra, in a 400-year-old townhouse, built by someone who thinks the best way to show love is to make sure you never run alone.

The Gozo Half Marathon winds through ancient villages, past the Marsalforn salt pans, along limestone cliffs, up hills that earn every view. Past Ta' Pinu Shrine, through Victoria's narrow streets, and back to Xagħra Square — where it all started.

This app knows every turn, every climb, every water station. It was made for one runner in particular — but it'll be there for every runner who needs it.

Run, Fiona. We'll be watching.

— with love, from the house on Triq il-Knisja 🦞
