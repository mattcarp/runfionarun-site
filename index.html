A Note

Made with Love
in Gozo

This app started as a simple idea: what if race day felt less like logistics and more like magic? What if your people could cheer you on from anywhere? What if every hill had a name and every view had a moment?

Gozo is a small island with a big heart. The half marathon winds through 5,000 years of history, past temples older than the pyramids, along cliffs that drop into impossibly blue water, through villages where everyone knows your name.

This app is for the runners and the people who love them. It's for the ones standing on the corner at KM 18, shouting themselves hoarse. It's for the ones tracking a dot on a map from three time zones away, willing it forward.

Run, Fiona. We'll be watching.

— with love, from the house on Triq il-Knisja 🦞
