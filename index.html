Built for Gozo

Everything you need on race day — and the weeks of training before it.

📍

Live GPS Tracking

Real-time position on the official race route. See exactly where you are on the 21.1km course through Gozo's stunning landscape.

🗣️

Voice KM Alerts

Audio announcements at every kilometre marker — pace, distance, elevation gained. No need to look at your phone.

👀

Spectator Mode

Your friends and family track you live on the map. They see your position, your pace, and can send you a cheer when you need it most.

📊

Elevation Profile

Interactive chart showing the course elevation from sea level to 144m. Know what's coming — the hills of Gozo don't surprise you twice.

🎨

Beautiful Themes

Four hand-crafted colour themes. Dark cyan for night runs, coral for sunsets, light mode for bright days, spectator mode for the crowd.

⏱️

Race Countdown

Days, hours, minutes until the starting gun. Plus water station markers, first aid points, and marshal positions on the map.
